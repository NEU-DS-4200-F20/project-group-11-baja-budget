Project Team 11: Baja Budget, DS4200 F20

Yevhen Horban, Brandon Archbald, Shaun Khundker

Service-Learning Course Project as part of DS 4200 F20: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Cody Dunne, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

In essence, we plan on using the financial data provided by the Northeastern Motorsports Club to visualize changes in their budget over time, and also show where they are spending the most money. We plan on using three different visualizations to display the spending of the NU Motorsports Club. We will be using D3 primarily. Click here for the link to our GitHub repository.
